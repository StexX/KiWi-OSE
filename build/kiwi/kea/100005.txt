 Queen Victoria and Her Age 
  

  
  
 
 

 
 





 




 If Alexandria Victoria (1819-1901), better known as Queen Victoria, had reigned a century later her life would have offered the media an endless source of fascinating  stories. Just imagine the press coverage a fun–loving, exuberant eighteen-year-old Queen would have faced. Her whole life would have played out in the limelight. And the headlines: “Young Impulsive Queen Falls Madly In Love With German Prince” -  “Tragic Death Of Prince Albert” -  “Broken-Hearted Queen” – “The Queen Neglects Her Duties” – “The Queen In Balmoral With John Brown” - Stop Press. Perhaps it was just as well for Victoria and her children that the paparazzi were not around in the nineteenth century.  Let’s take our own closer look at “Queen Victoria and Her Age”.
